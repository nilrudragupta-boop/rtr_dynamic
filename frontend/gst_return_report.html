GST Admin Best Practices

1. Reconciliation (GSTR-2A/2B vs Books)

Always reconcile the Input Tax Credit (ITC) claimed in your books (Purchase Register) with the GSTR-2B generated by the GST portal.

  • Match: Ensure Invoice No, Date, and GSTIN match exactly.
  • Mismatch: If a supplier hasn't filed, follow up immediately. You cannot claim ITC if it doesn't appear in GSTR-2B.
  • Formula:
    Net Payable = Output Tax (Sales) - Eligible Input Tax (Purchases)

2. Reverse Charge Mechanism (RCM)

For supplies from unregistered dealers or specific services (like GTA, Legal services), the recipient is liable to pay tax.

  • Create a "Self-Invoice" for such expenses.
  • Pay the tax in cash (cannot use ITC balance).
  • Claim the paid amount as ITC in the same month.

3. Zero-Rated Exports

Exports can be made either under Bond/LUT (without tax payment) or with payment of IGST (refundable).

  • With LUT: Taxable Value is reported, Tax Amount is 0.
  • With Payment: Pay IGST and claim refund. Ensure Shipping Bill details match GSTR-1.

4. Ineligible Credits (Section 17(5))

ITC is not available for:

  • Motor vehicles (seating capacity ≤ 13).
  • Food, beverages, outdoor catering, beauty treatment.
  • Goods lost, stolen, destroyed, or written off.
  • Goods used for personal consumption.
